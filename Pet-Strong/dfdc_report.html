BCS

Rufus’s BCS shows he is Overweight

  • An ideal BCS for a healthy dog should range between 4-5.

  • BCS higher than 5 can lead to joint issues, heart disease & more.

  • BCS can be lowered with the help of controlled diet & exercise.

Tips to ensure a BCS between 4-5

  • diet

    Gradually reduce food portions and consider switching to a weight management formula recommended by your veterinarian.

  • exercise

    Increase exercise duration and intensity gradually. Activities like swimming and brisk walking can be beneficial.

  • fresh water

    Always ensure your dog has access to fresh, clean water regardless of BCS.

  • vet consultation

    Discuss a safe weight loss plan with your veterinarian to avoid muscle loss.

  • mental stimulation

    Provide mental stimulation through interactive toys, training sessions, and enrichment activities.

  • Love and Attention

    Shower your dog with love and positive reinforcement during their weight management journey.

  • dental care

    Regular dental hygiene is important for all dogs, regardless of weight.
